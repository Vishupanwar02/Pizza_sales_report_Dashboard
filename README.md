
🍕 1. Project Title / Headline

Pizza Sales Performance & Revenue Analytics Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze pizza sales performance, customer ordering behavior, product trends, and revenue insights across 2015.
________________________________________
2. Short Description / Purpose
The Pizza Sales Dashboard is a dynamic and visually interactive Power BI report designed to analyze total revenue, order trends, product category performance, and customer purchasing patterns. It helps business owners, sales managers, and analysts understand peak sales periods, best-selling products, and size preferences to support data-driven business decisions.
________________________________________
3. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
•	📊 Power BI Desktop – Main data visualization and reporting platform.
•	📂 Power Query – Data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
•	🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for calculated measures such as Total Revenue, Average Order Value, and KPIs.
•	🔗 Data Modeling – Relationships established between sales, orders, and product category tables for cross-filtering and aggregation.
•	📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard preview.
________________________________________
4. Data Source
Source: Internal pizza sales transactional dataset (2015).
The dataset includes:
•	Order ID
•	Order Date
•	Pizza Category (Classic, Supreme, Chicken, Veggie)
•	Pizza Size (S, M, L, XL, XXL)
•	Quantity Sold
•	Revenue per Order
The data represents one full year of sales activity from January 2015 to December 2015.
________________________________________
5. Features / Highlights
• Business Problem
The pizza business generates thousands of transactions annually, but without proper analytics, it is difficult to answer key questions such as:
•	Which days generate the highest sales?
•	Which pizza category drives the most revenue?
•	What pizza size is most preferred?
•	Are there seasonal sales trends?
•	How can inventory and promotions be optimized?
Raw sales data alone does not provide quick, actionable insights.
________________________________________
• Goal of the Dashboard
To deliver an interactive analytics tool that:
•	Tracks revenue and order performance.
•	Identifies peak business days and months.
•	Analyzes product category contribution.
•	Understands customer size preferences.
•	Supports strategic decisions in marketing, staffing, and inventory planning.

🔹 Monthly Trend for Total Orders (Line Chart)
•	July is the peak sales month.
•	Strong performance in January and May.
•	Sales dip in September and October.

________________________________________
• Business Impact & Insights
📈 Revenue Optimization
Focus marketing efforts on Classic and Large-size pizzas to maximize profit.
🗓 Staffing & Operations Planning
Increase staffing and stock levels on Fridays and weekends.
📦 Inventory Management
Maintain higher inventory levels for Large-size pizza ingredients.
📊 Seasonal Strategy
Introduce promotions during September and October to counter lower sales.
🎯 Targeted Campaigns
Bundle offers for medium and small sizes to increase their contribution.
